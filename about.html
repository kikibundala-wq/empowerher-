Our Story

Built on the belief that biology shouldn't limit opportunity

EmpowerHer was founded to address period poverty in underserved South African communities, where many young girls miss school simply because they lack access to sanitary products.

Organisation overview

EmpowerHer is a nonprofit organisation working to enhance women's dignity, hygiene and health. We were founded to address the issue of women and girls in underprivileged communities not having access to feminine hygiene products.

Due to limited access to sanitary goods, many young girls miss school or experience discomfort. EmpowerHer distributes hygiene products and offers menstrual health education to address this problem, which affects girls and women across the country.

Mission

To empower women and girls by giving them access to reasonably priced and free feminine hygiene products, and educating them about health, dignity, and self-assurance.

Vision

A society in which a lack of resources does not — and should never — disadvantage any woman or girl.
