Our Mission

Empowering women and girls by giving them access to dignity.

We exist because limited access to sanitary products keeps young girls out of school and out of opportunity. EmpowerHer distributes hygiene products and menstrual health education to change that — one community at a time.

About Our Story
  • Free and reasonably priced hygiene products for those who need them most
  • Confidential, judgement-free assistance requests
  • Education that builds confidence, not just knowledge
  • A transparent, community-led approach to every donation
